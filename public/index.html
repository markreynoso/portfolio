About Mark

A few years ago I had an idea to make an app that would allow individuals to give electronically to folks experiencing homelessness. I talked with developer friends, pitched the idea to non-profits in the area, but it was not moving anywhere. Then one day while working at New Horizons, a service for homeless youth in Seattle, I ended up on the phone with someone who was making this very app. We met and I was elated to know that someone was able to make it happen. Through this process I realized that I wanted to be the one designing and building the app to solve this very real problem. So, here I am. Today I code, but I do it with the passion for making a positive impact in my community, solving problems with creative new solutions to real issues we face.
